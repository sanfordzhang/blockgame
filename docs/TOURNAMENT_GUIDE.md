## Overview

The tournament system allows players to compete in Texas Hold'em poker tournaments with real TRX stakes. Win prizes by outlasting your opponents!

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Connect Your Wallet

1. Visit the game website
2. Click **"Connect Wallet"** in the top right
3. Select **TronLink** from the options
4. Approve the connection request in your TronLink wallet
5. Ensure you're on the correct network (Testnet or Mainnet)

### Step 2: Deposit Funds

1. Navigate to **Wallet** page
2. Click **"Deposit"**
3. Enter the amount of TRX you want to deposit
4. Confirm the transaction in TronLink
5. Wait for confirmation (usually 1-3 minutes)

> **Note:** You need enough TRX to cover the tournament buy-in plus gas fees (~5 TRX per transaction).

### Step 3: Browse Tournaments

1. Navigate to **Tournament** page
2. View available tournaments in the lobby
3. Check the following details:
   - **Buy-in**: Entry cost in TRX
   - **Players**: Current/Maximum players
   - **Prize Pool**: Total prize amount
   - **Status**: WAITING, IN_PROGRESS, COMPLETED

### Step 4: Join a Tournament

1. Click on a tournament card to view details
2. Click **"Join Tournament"** button
3. Confirm the transaction in TronLink
4. You'll be charged the buy-in amount
5. Wait in the tournament lobby until full

> **Tip:** For 2-player tournaments, you only need one more player to join before the tournament starts!

## During the Tournament

### Game Controls

| Button | Action |
|--------|--------|
| **Fold** | Give up your hand |
| **Check** | Pass (if no bet required) |
| **Call** | Match the current bet |
| **Raise** | Increase the bet |
| **All-In** | Bet all your chips |

### Time Limits

- **Action Timeout:** 15 seconds to make your move
- **Time Bank:** 60 seconds total for extended thinking
- **Timeout Penalty:** Automatic Fold if you run out of time

> **Warning:** If you disconnect, you will automatically Fold your current hand!

### Tournament Rules

1. **Initial Chips:** Each player starts with equal chips (e.g., 10,000 for a 100 TRX buy-in)
2. **Blinds:** Fixed small blind and big blind throughout the tournament
3. **Elimination:** When you lose all chips, you're eliminated
4. **Winner:** Last player standing wins!

## Tips for Success

### Strategy Tips

1. **Position Matters:** Play tighter in early position, looser in late position
2. **Chip Management:** Don't risk all your chips on marginal hands
3. **Observe Opponents:** Watch for patterns in their play style
4. **Bluff Sparingly:** In small tournaments, bluffing is less effective

### Technical Tips

1. **Stable Connection:** Use a stable internet connection to avoid disconnection
2. **Battery Charged:** Ensure your device has enough battery
3. **Notifications Enabled:** Turn on sound/notification alerts for your turn
4. **Avoid Multitasking:** Don't play multiple games simultaneously during tournaments

## FAQ

### Can I leave a tournament after joining?

Yes, but only before the tournament starts:
1. Go to tournament details
2. Click **"Cancel Join"**
3. Your buy-in will be refunded

### What happens if the tournament doesn't fill?

For Sit & Go tournaments:
- If no other players join within a reasonable time, you can cancel and get a refund
- Contact support if you've been waiting for over 30 minutes

### What if I disconnect during a hand?

- Your hand will be automatically folded
- You remain in the tournament with your remaining chips
- Reconnect as soon as possible to continue playing

### Can I play in multiple tournaments at once?

No, you can only participate in one tournament at a time. This ensures fair play and prevents conflicts.

### How is the prize calculated?

```
Prize Pool = (Buy-in × Players) - (Buy-in × Players × Rake Rate)
```

Example: 2 players × 100 TRX × (100% - 5%) = 190 TRX prize pool

### Is there a minimum buy-in?

Minimum buy-in varies by tournament type:
- 2-player: 10 TRX minimum
- 3-player: 20 TRX minimum
- 6-player: 50 TRX minimum

## Troubleshooting

### Issue: Can't join tournament

**Possible causes:**
1. Insufficient balance - Deposit more TRX
2. Already in a tournament - Leave current tournament first
3. Wallet not connected - Reconnect your wallet

### Issue: Transaction pending

**Solution:**
1. Check TRON network status
2. Wait 3-5 minutes for confirmation
3. Refresh the page after confirmation

### Issue: Prize not showing

**Solution:**
1. Wait a few minutes for blockchain confirmation
2. Refresh the page
3. Check your wallet balance in the Wallet page
